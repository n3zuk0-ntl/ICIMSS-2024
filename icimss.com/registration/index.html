Registration

All presented and registered papers will be recommended for publication.

Registration Guidelines

Registered members are asked to provide intimation about their payments immediately via google form to

  • The registration fee is applicable per paper. Any of the authors can register by using the Acceptance ID.
  • After completion of registration, every participant is required to send a scanned copy of the registration fee receipt or transaction proof to us immediately via email.
  • Any modification in the paper will not be accepted after the final submission date.
  • After Payment, send your final updated paper and filled-in publishing agreement to Google Form
  • After registration, cancellation/alterations are not possible.
